Foam Pet Rules (Originals)

	

		
Description

		

			Foam Pets are loyal, friendly, and scholarly.  
			Please treat them with loyalty, friendliness, and scholarliness.
		

	

	

		
Festivities

		

			Festivities with Foam Pets are allowed 
			if the Foam Pet friend is a loyal friend
			to Foam Pet and the friends of Foam Pet.
		

	

	

		
Reflections

		

			Any of these is allowed for 
			any friendly or festive purposes.

			Making unmixed or mixed reflections
			of Foam Pet for friends is allowed.
			
			Also, making unmixed or mixed 
			reflections of Foam Pet parts 
			for friends is allowed.
