Foam Pet Rules (Originals)

	

		
Description

		

			Foam Pets are loyal, friendly, and scholarly.  
			Please treat them with loyalty, friendliness, and scholarliness.
		

	

	

		
Festivities

		

			Festivities with Foam Pets are allowed so
			long as the friend is a loyal friend
			to Foam Pet and the friends of Foam Pet.
		

	

	

		
Cloning

		

			Any of these is allowed for 
			any friendly or festive purposes.

			Cloning the entire pet or parts of the pet
			is allowed.

			Making pet mixes for friends that feature 
			any of these is permitted:
				"(unremixed) clones of parts"
				"(unremixed) clones of the pet"			
				"remixed clones of parts",
				"remixed clones of the pet",
